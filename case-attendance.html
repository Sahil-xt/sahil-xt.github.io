RoleLead UX Designer

Step 1 — Empathise

Five different people. Five different problems.

👔
Owner/HR Admin
Sets policy, monitors real-time
Pain: No visibility into device misuse, complex policy management
Need: One-click policy setup, real-time dashboard
👨‍💼
HR Manager
Research rules, process requests
Pain: Complex rules, manual approvals, time spent on research
Need: Plain-language policies, auto request handling, batch approval
👨‍🔧
Field Manager
Blue collar supervision
Pain: No device = can't clock in, bulk approvals daily, hidden absenteeism
Need: Paper + kiosk fallback, one-tap bulk actions, live alerts
💼
White Collar Employee
Desktop-based, needs grace periods
Pain: Strict rules = no flexibility, late arrival penalties, timezone confusion
Need: 5-min grace period, self-regularisation, clear time display
🔧
Blue Collar Employee
Kiosk + paper, language barriers
Pain: No smartphone, language barriers, can't self-fix
Need: Face-scan kiosk, icons over text, request regularisation

Key insight: Policy complexity, not tracking, was the real problem. HR spent weeks managing overlapping rules. Solution: Decouple policy from rules, make policies modular and reusable.
