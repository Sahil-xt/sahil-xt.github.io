Case 02 — Leave Management

Leave policy -- year-end automation wins

Modular policies + auto carry-forward + team availability calendar

RoleLead UX Designer
PlatformWeb + Mobile
Scale500k+ employees
Step 1 — Empathise

December was a nightmare.

Surveyed 500+ companies. Unanimous: December (year-end leave) was the most stressful month. HR spent 3-4 weeks on manual carry-forward calculations, chasing manager approvals, fixing compliance issues.

🏢
Owner
Policy oversight, compliance
Pain: Carry-forward complexity, compliance risk, manual processing
Need: Auto year-end processing, clear rules, audit trail
👩‍💼
HR Manager
Approvals, year-end processing
Pain: Month-long bottleneck, delayed manager approvals, late declarations
Need: Auto reminders, batch approvals, auto calculations
👔
Team Manager
Approves requests, plans coverage
Pain: Too many requests, no team availability view, last-minute surprises
Need: Calendar view, batch approval, advance visibility
👤
Employee
Requests leave, checks balance
Pain: Unclear balance, slow approvals, no clarity on carry-forward
Need: Live balance, fast decisions, plain-language policies

Key insight: Year-end carry-forward calculations were discovered wrong in November. Too late to fix without reconciliation. Solution: Automate before the problem occurs.

Step 2 — Define

Four problems at different times of year:

📋
Policy Confusion
Complex, overlapping rules. Employees don't know balance.
⏳
Year-End Backlog
Manual carry-forward calculations. Compliance reviews. Declarations trickling in.
📅
Coverage Blind Spot
Managers approve one-by-one. No team-wide availability view.
📱
Communication Lag
Blue collar workers only get SMS. No app transparency.
